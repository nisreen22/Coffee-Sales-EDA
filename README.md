# ☕ Coffee Sales: From Raw Data to Strategic Insights

## 📖 The Story
In a competitive coffee market, understanding customer behavior and product performance is the key to growth. This project isn't just about numbers; it's about uncovering the story behind every cup sold. By analyzing sales data across the **US, UK, and Ireland**, we aimed to answer one critical question: **"What truly drives our profit?"**

---

## 🛠️ The Craft (Process)
Every great story needs a solid foundation. Here’s how I prepared the data:

*   **Data Refinement (Python):** Using **Pandas**, I transformed raw, "noisy" CSV files into a clean, structured format. This included handling missing values, standardizing date formats, and normalizing international data.
*   **The Discovery (EDA):** I performed Exploratory Data Analysis to find hidden patterns. I found a **96% correlation** between size and pricing, ensuring our price strategy remains consistent across borders.

---

## 📊 The Visual Narrative (Dashboard)
To make the data speak to decision-makers, I built an interactive **Power BI Dashboard**. 

### **Key Highlights of the Dashboard:**
*   **Executive Summary:** A bird’s-eye view of Total Revenue and our growing Customer Base.
*   **Geospatial Performance:** A **Filled Map** visualizing our dominance in the US and identifying emerging opportunities in Europe.
*   **The Loyalty Chapter:** A deep dive into how **Loyalty Cards** impact the bottom line. 

> 💡 **Note:** You can find a screenshot of the dashboard in the `Dashboard/` folder.

---

## 💡 The "Aha!" Moment (Key Insights)
After digging through the data, three major stories emerged:
1.  **Loyalty Gap:** Surprisingly, customers with loyalty cards currently spend less compared to those without cards. This identifies a major opportunity to redesign our loyalty incentives to boost high-value spending.
2.  **Product Heroes:** Certain coffee origins (like **Excelsa**) show higher profit margins despite lower sales volume compared to Arabian blends.
3.  **Size Matters:** The **2.5kg (LARGE)** package is our "Value King," driving the highest revenue in the residential segment.
